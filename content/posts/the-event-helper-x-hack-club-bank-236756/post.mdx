Hey fellow Bankers!

Every event needs a great venue and with a great venue comes insurance requirements... but where do you start?! A Google search lands you so many options and probably more questions than when you started...

![image.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/090/771-db2a4b3043a7177a1a938db02f12b461fa0c9f67.png =100%)

Partnering with [The Event Helper](https://www.theeventhelper.com/), we’ve created a streamlined process for you to purchase event insurance on Bank without the hassle. You now have access to a simplified, custom form in your “Perks” tab, and once submitted, Bank team will provide you with a custom quote. All of the costs are directly from The Event Helper – we don't charge a fee on top!
