# FIRST Worlds

The HCB team just got back from the FIRST Robotics Championship in Houston a few days ago, and it was a blast! HCB supports nearly 2,000 FIRST teams. We had a great time connecting with teams there and sharing more about what we do!

# Changelog

## Features

### ↕️ Sort your invoices

- Your invoices page just got a lot easier to scan. You can now sort invoices by clicking the table headers. <BlogPostLink slug="sort-your-invoices" />

  <Author id="manu" />

### 🔔 Toggle monthly donation and follower summary emails

- You can now toggle whether you receive monthly donation and follower summary emails from your notification settings. <BlogPostLink slug="summary-notification-settings" />

  <Author id="luke" />

### 🔔 Get notified when important settings change

- HCB will now send an email to all team members of an organization when transparency mode or monthly announcements are enabled or disabled. We hope this helps you keep your team in the loop! <BlogPostLink slug="organization-settings-notifications" />

  <Author id="samuel" />

## Small changes

- All items in the command bar now match the icons and order of the items in the left-side navigation. <PullRequestLink number="13265" />

  <Author id="luke" />

- Generate and disable TOTP buttons now appear as buttons and not plain text. <PullRequestLink number="13381" />

  <Author id="luke" />

- The agreement signing page for teen applications to HCB is now clearer about the application process. <PullRequestLink number="13456" />

  <Author id="samuel" />

- The "Have you applied for fiscal sponsorship with HCB in the past?" question in the HCB application is now prefilled from our data. <PullRequestLink number="13457" />

  <Author id="samuel" />

- The team member invite modal now specifies that members can view transactions and finances. <PullRequestLink number="13522" />

  <Author id="manu" />

- The application review page is now clearer about why an application may not be ready to submit. <PullRequestLink number="13542" />

  <Author id="samuel" />

- CSV transaction exports now include the amount in both cents and dollars. <PullRequestLink number="13592" />

  <Author id="anish" />

## Bugfixes

- Pressing "Sign out everywhere else" now only updates currently signed in sessions.
  Before, it would incorrectly update the timestamp on already expired sessions. <PullRequestLink number="13375" />

  <Author id="luke" />

- Donations one-pager is now hidden if there are any regular donations or recurring donations.
  Previously, if there were only recurring donations you would be unable to view them. <PullRequestLink number="13384" />

  <Author id="luke" />

- Card names are now shown on the [My cards](https://hcb.hackclub.com/my/cards) page.
  Previously, only card grant purposes were visible here. <PullRequestLink number="13410" />

  <Author id="luke" />

- The donation goal reached and invite accepted emails are now clearer about who can create an announcement. <PullRequestLink number="13444" />

  <Author id="samuel" />

- Contrast when hovering over orange colored buttons in dark mode is now fixed. <PullRequestLink number="13523" />

  <Author id="manu" />

- If your applications have all been archived, HCB will now redirect you to the new application page instead of an archived application. <PullRequestLink number="13536" />

  <Author id="samuel" />
