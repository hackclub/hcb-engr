# FIRST Worlds

The HCB team just got back from the FIRST Robotics Championship in Houston a few days ago, and it was a blast! We had a great time connecting with teams and sharing more about what we do. For context, HCB supports nearly 2,000 FIRST teams, with the number growing by the day.

# Changelog

## Features

### ↕️ Sort your invoices

Your invoices page just got a lot easier to scan. You can now sort invoices by clicking the table headers. <BlogPostLink slug="sort-your-invoices" />

<Author id="manu" />

### 🔔 Toggle monthly donation and follower summary emails

From your notification settings, you can now toggle whether you receive monthly donation and follower summary emails. <BlogPostLink slug="summary-notification-settings" />

<Author id="luke" />

### 🔔 Get notified when important settings change

HCB will now send an email to all team members of an organization when transparency mode or monthly announcements are enabled or disabled. We hope this helps you keep your team in the loop! <BlogPostLink slug="organization-settings-notifications" />

<Author id="samuel" />

## Bugfixes

- Pressing "Sign out everywhere else" now only updates currently signed-in sessions.
  Previously, it would incorrectly update the timestamp on already-expired sessions. <PullRequestLink number="13375" />

  <Author id="luke" />

- The donations one-pager is now hidden whenever any donations exist, one-time or recurring.
  Previously, if you only had recurring donations, the one-pager would still appear and you couldn't view them. <PullRequestLink number="13384" />

  <Author id="luke" />

- Card names are now shown on the [My cards](https://hcb.hackclub.com/my/cards) page.
  Previously, only card grant purposes were visible here. <PullRequestLink number="13410" />

  <Author id="luke" />

- The donation goal reached and invite accepted emails are now clearer about who can create an announcement. <PullRequestLink number="13444" />

  <Author id="samuel" />

- Hover contrast on orange buttons in dark mode is now fixed. <PullRequestLink number="13523" />

  <Author id="manu" />

- If all of your applications have been archived, HCB will now redirect you to the new application page instead of an archived one. <PullRequestLink number="13536" />
