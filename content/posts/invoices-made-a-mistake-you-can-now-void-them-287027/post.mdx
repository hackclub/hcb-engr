You can now void your invoices if you make a mistake in filling out a field, or if the invoice is no longer needed (like if it's overdue)!

![Picture of the invoice page showing a red button that says "Void this invoice"](https://cloud-808d4v1y2-hack-club-bot.vercel.app/0screenshot_2024-03-18_at_8.51.18___am.png)

Just click the big red "Void this Invoice" button on an invoice and it'll be voided permanently.
