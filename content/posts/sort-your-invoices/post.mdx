Your invoices page just got a lot easier to scan. You can now sort invoices by clicking the table headers.

Click the same column again to switch between ascending and descending order, so it is easier to find the oldest invoice, the largest one, or group similar statuses together at a glance.

We're starting with invoices first, and we plan to bring the same sorting controls to more parts of HCB soon!
