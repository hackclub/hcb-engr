- 🎨 We've refreshed our colors and icons for a modern experience.

**Remember when logging into HCB just showed you a list of transactions?** *Yeah, we thought that could be better.*

Introducing the new HCB Home – your command center for everything happening in your organization.

- 📊 Know exactly where you stand the moment you log in, complete with a visual graph showing your balance over time.
- ⏰ See latest activity, right on the home page. No more hunting through settings to see what just happened.
- 🚀 See what your team is up to in real-time. Transfers, comments, and updates all in one place.
- 🙏🏼 Avatars showing who's part of your organization, plus key stats like total revenue.
- 💸 Transfer money, get reimbursed, deposit checks, and more – all accessible from the top of your dashboard.
