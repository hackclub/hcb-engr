### One unified navigation, everywhere

Previously, we had two separate navigation systems: one for desktop and one for mobile. This led to strange inconsistencies and bugs depending on which device you used.
Now, we use the **exact same navigation** on both desktop and mobile. On a larger screen you’ll see it as the familiar sidebar; on mobile, that same sidebar slides in as a drawer.
